My Journey into Tech

Hey, my name is Nicole! I started in tech in highschool making HTML websites for fun. After college, I landed my first big girl job in 2016 working at a medical billing clearinghouse as a Customer Service representative.

In August 2021, I started my first Youtube channel where I showcased my musical talents as well as skills in photography and videography. In August 2021 I landed a role at Apple Inc. as an Associate Producer. I accepted the offer and moved from the Midwest to California. I currently reside in Silicon Valley.

What I've learned during this journey is that hardwork and having faith in yourself can get you far in life. I now believe there is nothing I can't do.
